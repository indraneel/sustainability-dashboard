Our mission:

Sustainable Jersey empowers communities to pursue sustainability programs. At Dashability, we show you exactly how well those programs are doing. Dashability’s intuitive visual accounting of sustainable accomplishments helps communities to organize and share their progress with all sorts of audiences.

What are we trying to solve?

The biggest problem that communities face in pursuing a sustainability program is generating buy-in and participation. Whether advocating for a new program to the city council or simply showing citizens how their towns have become more sustainable, local Green Teams need a tool to quickly and clearly explain the impact of their actions to stakeholders. Dashability offers a two-pronged solution. We give communities an intuitive, measurable, and shareable platform to demonstrate the value of their sustainability missions, and pair it with built-in connections to Facebook, Twitter, and other social media sites so they can share their work with their existing networks instead of building new followings from scratch.

3 Easy Steps

Load Information

Convert your Sustainable Jersey actions using our straightforward visualization format.

View

Visualize your actions as cards, each with its own story, presented both individually and within broader categories.

Share

Promote your actions directly to your community through our social media tie-ins.
